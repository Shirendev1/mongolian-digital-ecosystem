# mongolian-digital-ecosystem
Concept project exploring a Mongolian digital ecosystem based on the .mn domain, supporting Mongolian language presence and digital infrastructure on the global internet.
This project explores the idea of building a stronger Mongolian digital ecosystem based on the .mn domain.

Goals
- Strengthen Mongolia’s digital presence online
- Support Mongolian language on the internet
- Explore national digital services such as email and internet portals
- Encourage discussion within the global internet governance community

Status
Concept stage – open for discussion and collaboration.
